Microsoft Office 365

Microsoft Office 365 is the IT solution for your business. You can organize your e-mails, schedules, calendars and documents with ease anytime, anywhere – Office 365 is online 24/7 and is accessible on any computer and most handheld devices. Microsoft Office 365 also has the latest protection against spam and viruses, as well as a enterprise-grade reliability and disaster recovery guarantee to keep your data safe. At Goldline IT we can help you set up your business with Microsoft Office 365 so you can keep it organised and running efficiently while we manage your IT networks.

Medical IT Solutions

Over the last decade, the medical industry has embraced the addition of IT based systems in their daily operations, simplifying their databases, schedules and procedures while increasing in efficiency and reliability. We understand that each medical practice is unique and requires a highly dynamic IT support structure to ensure the highest levels of efficiency and safety within the practice, and at Goldline IT we can offer experienced medical IT support to help you manage your IT systems. We support HCN, Medical Director, Parasoft, Best Practice, ZedMed, Genie and a host of other medical applications.

Mailguard

Goldline IT can help you set up your business with MailGuard to provide email protection, filtering and archiving without the need for you to worry about the technical side of things.
